The World of Aethelgard

The Crystal Spires

Rising from the mists of the Silver Lake, the Crystal Spires have stood for a thousand years, guarding the ancient secrets of the First Mages. It is said that the light reflected from them can reveal one's true destiny.

The Shadowlands

Beyond the Iron Mountains lies a land where the sun never fully rises. The Shadowlands are home to creatures of nightmare and the banished souls of the Betrayal War.

The Guild of Whispers

Not all power is wielded with swords or spells. The Guild of Whispers controls the flow of information across the kingdoms, their spies hidden in every court and tavern.

The Blacksmith's Daughter

"The fire didn't burn her. That was the first sign. When the ember fell onto her cradle, the wood charred, but the babe only giggled, reaching for the dancing flame with chubby fingers..."

As she grew, so did the rumors. They whispered that she had dragon's blood, that her father had made a pact with a fire demon. But old man Horgus just hammered away at his anvil, his eyes sad and knowing. He knew the truth was far more dangerous than any village gossip.

On her sixteenth nameday, the King's inquisitors came. They didn't knock. The door exploded inward in a shower of splinters. "Where is the witch?" the captain bellowed, his armor gleaming in the forge-light. Horgus stepped forward, hammer in hand. "There are no witches here," he rumbled. "Only iron and blood."

The Last Sentinel

"Snow had been falling for three weeks. The pass was blocked, the supplies were low, and something was scratching at the heavy oak doors of the watchtower..."

Garrick tightened his grip on his spear. He was the last one left. The others had been taken, one by one, in the night. No screams, just silence where a breathing man had been moments before. The scratching stopped. The silence that followed was heavy, pressing against his ears like deep water.

Then, a voice. Not from the door, but from the shadows behind him. "It is cold outside, young sentinel. May I come in?"
